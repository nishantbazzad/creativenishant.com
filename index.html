About me

Hello there!

I’m Nishant Bazzad, an independent designer based in India with over 8 years of experience. I specialize in creating impactful digital experiences that resonate with brands and their audiences. My journey into design began in 2014, driven by curiosity, and it quickly evolved into a passion for crafting intuitive digital products. As a designer, I believe in the power of good design and the tangible value it can bring to a brand's identity and user experience.
What sets me apart? It’s my dedication to creating meaningful work. I deeply care about collaborating closely with my clients to ensure that every project reflects their vision and objectives. Outside of design, I enjoy reading, traveling, and contributing to philanthropic efforts.
