FAQ's

How long do projects usually take to complete?

The duration of the project depends on the scope of work. During my discovery phase, I take the time to gather information about you and your project through a free introductory call. Need something quick? I can design an MVP (Minimum Viable Product) within a timeline that suits your needs. As always, my goal is to create a quality product in a reasonable timeframes.

How much does a Product design project cost?

For any project, the cost is determined by the scope, timeline, and number of deliverables. My estimates are calculated based on my hourly rate and the estimated number of hours required for the job. Ongoing design support packages start from $1000/month with no long-time commitment. Feel free to contact me to receive a quote and learn more about my pricing structure.

What deliverables will I receive?

I use Figma for design, you will receive Figma file for every stage of the design process. A typical deliverables package consists of a low-fidelity wireframes Figma file, high fidelity wireframes Figma file, and a separate file containing all reusable components from the design.

This is great! How do I get started?

Email me or fill out the form. I'll get back to you within 1-2 business days. From there, we can schedule a call to discuss about the scope, pricing, and timeline.
